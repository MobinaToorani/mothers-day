Dear Mom,

There are feelings that words can hardly contain — and the way I love you is one of them. But here I am, trying anyway, because you deserve every letter, every sentence, every quiet breath of gratitude.

You have been my safe harbor in every storm. You held my hand through my darkest moments and cheered the loudest at every triumph. Your love is the invisible thread woven through every good thing in my life.

I see you in the patience I try to bring to hard days. I see you in the warmth I extend to others. I see you in the small kindnesses I offer without thinking — because you taught me, by example, that love is a verb. It is something you *do*, endlessly and without expectation.

Thank you for the sleepless nights I never knew about. Thank you for the worries you carried silently so I could sleep peacefully. Thank you for showing up, every single time, even when it was hard.

Thank you for being exactly who you are — perfectly, beautifully, irreplaceably you.

On this Mother's Day and every day that follows, I want you to know: you are my greatest gift, my brightest light, and my truest home.

With all the love I have and more,

*Your daughter* 🌸
